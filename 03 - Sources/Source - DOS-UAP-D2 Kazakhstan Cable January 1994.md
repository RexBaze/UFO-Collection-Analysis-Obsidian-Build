## Summary

U.S. Embassy Dushanbe cable to State Department reporting that Tajik Air Chief Pilot Captain Ed Rhodes (American citizen) and his two American pilot colleagues (all former PanAm pilots) had observed a UAP for 40 minutes from a Boeing 747SP at FL410 over Kazakhstan on 27 January 1994. The cable was disseminated to CIA, DIA, and multiple regional embassies.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "THEY WATCHED THE OBJECT FOR SOME FORTY MINUTES AS IT MANEUVERED IN CIRCLES, CORKSCREWS AND MADE 90-DEGREE TURNS AT RAPID RATES OF SPEED AND UNDER VERY HIGH G'S."

> [!QUOTE]
> "SOME FORTY-FIVE MINUTES AFTER THE INITIAL SIGHTING, AS THE SUN WAS RISING, THE AIRCRAFT FLEW UNDER THE CONTRAILS WHICH THE OBJECT HAD LEFT BEHIND. THE PLANE WAS MAKING OVER 500 KNOTS. RHODES ESTIMATED THE ALTITUDE OF THE CONTRAILS AT APPROXIMATELY 100,000 FEET."

> [!QUOTE]
> "ON THE BASIS OF ITS SPEED AND MANEUVERABILITY, RHODES EXPRESSED THE OPINION, WHICH HIS CREW SEEMED TO SUPPORT, THAT THE OBJECT WAS EXTRATERRESTRIAL AND UNDER INTELLIGENT CONTROL."

> [!QUOTE]
> "COMMENT: WE HAVE NO OPINION AND REPORT THE ABOVE FOR WHAT IT MAY BE WORTH."
