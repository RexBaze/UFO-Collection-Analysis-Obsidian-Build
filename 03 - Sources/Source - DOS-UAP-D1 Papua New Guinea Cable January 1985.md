## Summary

U.S. Embassy Port Moresby cable to USCINCPAC reporting a PNG National Intelligence Organization (NIO) inquiry regarding multiple sightings of high-altitude, high-speed aircraft over Papua New Guinea on the evening of 24 January 1985. Embassy confirmed no U.S. aircraft activity and requested additional information.
