## Summary

Two FBI FD-302 interview records (FD-302-67 and FD-302-71) of two witnesses describing the same Northeastern U.S. red-orb event in 2026. D009: the witness arrived home ~9:15pm EST and saw a red sphere ~1 meter across with a basketball-sized "white plasma sun" core; a second identical orb appeared and both moved west in tandem before merging into one, silent and smooth. Video was attached. Weeks later, several high white orbs crossed west-to-east, "size of a dime held at arm's length." D010: a second witness described yellow basketball- then beach-ball-sized lights ~30 yards out at 20-30 ft altitude with no health effects, one orb "going into the other orb before disappearing," and recounted a 1987 power-line sighting.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> (D009) a red sphere with a "white plasma sun" core; "never seen anything that color of red before."

> [!QUOTE]
> (D010) "one orb going into the other orb before disappearing."

The two FD-302s corroborate one another on the ~9:15pm arrival, the paired red orbs, the merging behavior, and the later white west-to-east orbs.
