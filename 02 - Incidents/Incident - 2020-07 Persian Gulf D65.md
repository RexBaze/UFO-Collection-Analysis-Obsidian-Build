## Assessment

==Unresolved with strong operational context.== Three separate UAP observations during a single 21-hour mission, ==while explicitly tasked to monitor UAS activity from Iranian naval forces==. This is one of the most contextually loaded incidents in the collection.

The candidate explanations narrow considerably given the mission tasking:

1. ==Iranian small UAS launched from IRGC-N vessels== (consistent with both the tasking and the geographic clustering)
2. Iranian larger UAS launched from coastal sites
3. Coalition or other-nation drones operating in the same airspace
4. Conventional aircraft mis-IDed
5. Sensor artifacts (less likely given 3 separate events on FMV)

The fact that the same crew flying the same sensor in the same general area saw 3 UAPs over 5+ hours suggests either persistent local activity or a sensor/processing bias of some kind. Without the FMV itself, this is the best the unclassified record permits.
