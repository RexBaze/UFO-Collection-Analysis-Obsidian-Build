## Narrative

Per a SECRET//NOFORN CIA cable, on the afternoon of 2 July 2008 an unidentified object was observed - possibly by both radar and optical means - hovering at high altitude directly over Harare International Airport. It was described as disc-like with a hollow center and a series of rotating underside lights; "beams" were seen emanating from it. The lights shifted color and the object ascended rapidly out of visual range. Officials could not determine whether it was an advanced foreign reconnaissance platform or "an unidentified flying object of extraterrestrial origins," and the event contributed to placing Zimbabwe on high alert. The cable reached a wide U.S. audience including the White House Situation Room.

> [!QUOTE]
> "the object was disc-like in shape with a hollow center, and had a series of rotating lights on the underside."

## Assessment

==Unresolved==. Among the stronger "structured craft" reports in the collection because of the claimed dual (radar + optical) detection and the seniority of its distribution. Caveats: the account is a single-source intelligence cable relaying foreign-official observations, with no released sensor data. Conventional candidates (aircraft with rotating beacon, atmospheric/astronomical effect) sit uneasily with the reported radar correlation and structured morphology. Connects the modern record to the historical disc reports of 1948-1968.
