## Summary

A 2004 U.S. State Department cable from Embassy Ashgabat (Turkmenistan), subject "TURKMENISTAN, CIVIL SOCIETY AND UFOS." The cable is a civil-society reporting item rather than a sighting report: it describes the "Union of UFOlogists (UOU) of Turkmenabat," a registered NGO in Lebap Welayet, and uses the group as a lens on how Turkmenistan's 2003 NGO law affects civil-society organizations. The cable explicitly notes there have been no confirmed UFO sightings in Turkmenistan.

## Key extractions

> 1. Yes, UFOs. Somehow, over time, the Union of UFOlogists of Turkmenabat has established a reputation as [an NGO]... working on... international UFO fora and published on the subject, but [there have] been no confirmed sightings of UFOs in Turkmenistan.
