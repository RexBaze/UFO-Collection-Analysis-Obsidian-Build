## Notes

This crew is unusual in the historical record because LMP Jack Schmitt is the only trained geologist who has walked on the Moon. His observation of a lunar surface flash near Grimaldi has more weight than an equivalent observation by a non-scientist crew member because he immediately framed the observation in testable scientific terms (impact hypothesis, suggested seismometer verification).
