## Assessment

==Probable conventional.== The report itself splits the five objects: one with a missile-like visual reconnaissance signature and four matching a possible-bird profile. Dust degraded the FMV. This is a useful baseline for how a single ISR feed can generate several heterogeneous UAP calls in one window, most of which the screeners themselves lean toward conventional.
