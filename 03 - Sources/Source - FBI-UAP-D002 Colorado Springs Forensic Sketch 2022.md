## Summary

FBI FD-1057 electronic communication documenting a July 2024 forensic-sketch interview at 26 Federal Plaza, New York, conducted by a Special Agent and an Operational Projects Unit (OPU) forensic artist with the primary witness, a former U.S. Army intelligence officer. It records the witness's description used to build a forensic sketch and notes an initial sketch draft was enclosed, with further drafts to follow to complete the final product.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "The object was 'potato' shaped with distinct edges and appeared to look painted in a creamy/whitish opalescent color."

> [!QUOTE]
> "articulating fish scales or panels that were non-symmetrical, non-overlapping... each panel on the object shifted in slow waves."

> [!QUOTE]
> "the object 'cloaked' in the space of time it took to turn a head and there was no shadow."

The witness described "the perfect 'blue bird' sky," no clouds, ~50 deg F - a direct contradiction of the cloud conditions the [[Source - ICA-UAP-D001 Colorado Springs Analysis|ICA assessment]] relies on.
