## Assessment

Both events are ==unresolved==. Event 1 (single object at 40 kts at FL165) has a slow speed profile consistent with balloons, slow drones, or even birds at altitude. Event 2 (two objects at 278 kts accelerating south) is closer to small-aircraft profile. The two events ~9 hours apart in the same general area suggest persistent activity, either of recurring objects or of recurring observation conditions.
