## Summary

Email correspondence between an Information Disclosure Analyst at the Office of the Undersecretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security and a PAROC Intel Data Analysis Technician Team Lead at [[Unit - 12 AF DET 3]] confirming that two tearlines describing UAP sightings on 10 and 11 April 2025 in the [[Location - INDOPACOM AOR]] are approved for release at the unclassified level. AOR confirmed as INDOPACOM at the unclassified level.
