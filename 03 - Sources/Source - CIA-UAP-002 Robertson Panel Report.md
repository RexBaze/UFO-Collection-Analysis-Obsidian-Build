## Summary

The CIA-held copy of the 1953 Scientific Advisory Panel on Unidentified Flying Objects (the Robertson Panel) report and its transmittal letters to the Secretary of Defense and Federal Civil Defense Administration. Convened under DCI Walter Bedell Smith and chaired by physicist H.P. Robertson, the panel reviewed the best available UFO cases and concluded the phenomena posed no direct national-security threat, but warned of indirect dangers (clogged reporting channels, public susceptibility) and recommended a program of public "education" and de-emphasis. It became the policy foundation for Air Force Blue Book's debunking posture. The DOW transmittal copy is [[Source - DOW-UAP-D085 Robertson Panel Transmission 1953]].
