## Summary

A 13 December 1948 message from the Commandant, Fifth Naval District (Norfolk, VA), "Flying Discs - Report of." It relays that the Director of Intelligence, USAF, informed the Navy of "a cycle of recurrence of 'Flying Discs'" and asked that all Naval stations report sightings as incidents, with photographs where possible. Signed "by direction" (Thos. R. Inglis) and R.C. Hudson, Chief of Staff. Documents USAF-Navy coordination on disc reporting during the Project Sign era.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "a cycle of recurrence of 'Flying Discs' is becoming apparent."

> [!QUOTE]
> requests that "photographic evidence... be obtained."
