## Notes

Over two days in October 2023 the agents, working in two-person teams, independently reported the same signature: bright orange "mother orbs" that hovered silently and expelled clusters of 3-4 smaller red "orbs" into evenly-spaced horizontal lines, paired with horizon flashes, plus ground-level light objects that mimicked vehicles, hovered without raising dust, and evaded on approach. Their five first-hand narratives ([[Source - DOW-UAP-D079-D083 Western US Event Agent Narratives]]) corroborate one another on the expulsion sequence, the airfield "square" of red lights, and a mismatched red-round/white-square "vehicle" object seen during the drive-out. Several agents deliberately used rangefinders and night-vision gear and offered careful distance hedging, which AARO cited as a credibility factor.
