## Narrative

In early 2026, two witnesses described the same evening event at the Northeastern U.S. residence, recorded in February 2026 FBI FD-302 interviews. The primary witness arrived home ~9:15pm EST and saw a red sphere roughly 1 meter across with a basketball-sized white "plasma sun" core; a second identical orb appeared and the two moved west in tandem, silent and smooth, before merging into one. Video was captured. Weeks later, several high white orbs crossed west-to-east ("size of a dime held at arm's length"). The second witness described yellow basketball- to beach-ball-sized lights ~30 yards out at 20-30 ft altitude, one orb "going into the other orb before disappearing."

## Assessment

==Unresolved==. Two witnesses corroborate the arrival time, paired red orbs, merging behavior, and later white west-to-east orbs, and a video was captured (not yet correlated to a released file). The "plasma core" and "merging" behaviors lack a clean conventional analogue; candidate explanations include drones with illuminated payloads, distant aircraft/celestial misperception, or camera artifacts. This event continues the multi-year Northeastern U.S. case begun in [[Incident - 2024 Northeastern US Plasma Orbs]].
