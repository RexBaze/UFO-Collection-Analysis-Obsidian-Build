## Notes

The five soldiers observed the object over the saddle of Cheyenne Mountain on 15 February 2022 while walking west toward a building entrance. They watched for an estimated 3-5 minutes (the FBI FD-302) to ~2 minutes (the FD-1057 sketch interview); none had a phone to hand, and the object vanished the moment they looked away to retrieve one. Each independently sketched a horizontal, white, "bean/potato-shaped" object with abstract intersecting lines; the drawings were mutually consistent. The primary witness reported being contacted by AARO in early 2024 but said he was never formally interviewed before the FBI engaged in 2025.
