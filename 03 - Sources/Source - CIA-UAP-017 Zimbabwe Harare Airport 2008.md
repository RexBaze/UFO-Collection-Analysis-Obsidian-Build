## Summary

A SECRET//NOFORN CIA cable, distributed to a wide U.S. military and IC audience (including the White House Situation Room, DNI, DIA, NSA), reporting that on the afternoon of 2 July 2008 an unidentified object was observed - "possibly by both radar and optical means" - hovering at high altitude directly over Harare International Airport, Zimbabwe. Observers described it as disc-like with a hollow center and rotating underside lights; "beams" were reportedly seen emanating from it. The lights shifted color and the object ascended rapidly out of visual range. Officials were uncertain whether it was an advanced foreign reconnaissance device or "an unidentified flying object of extraterrestrial origins"; the incident contributed to placing Zimbabwe on high alert.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "the object was disc-like in shape with a hollow center, and had a series of rotating lights on the underside."

One of the strongest "hard UAP" items in tranche 3: a structured-disc report with claimed dual (radar + optical) detection that reached the highest levels of the U.S. government.
