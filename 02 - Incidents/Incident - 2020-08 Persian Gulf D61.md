## Assessment

==Unresolved==. The formation profile (multiple objects in coordinated flight) is more constrained than single-object observations. Possible explanations:

1. ==Iranian formation flight== (could be multiple small UAS, multiple aircraft on training, or a group of UAVs returning to base)
2. Bird flock (would be challenging to misidentify on an EO/IR sensor at that range)
3. Coordinated balloon release (low probability)
4. Sensor processing artifact (low probability given 2-minute coherent track)

The temporal pattern (~2 minute observation broken by clouds, no reacquisition) is consistent with active platforms continuing their flight path while sensor LOS was occluded.
