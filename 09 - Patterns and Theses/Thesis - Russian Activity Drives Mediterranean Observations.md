## Hypothesis

The Mediterranean cluster of U.S. military UAP observations correlates with Russian Navy and aviation activity in the Eastern Mediterranean, especially during the 2015-2017 Russian Syria deployment and recurring KCTG (Kuznetsov Carrier Task Group) operations.

## Reasoning

1. The 2016 P-8A Latakia observation is assessed in the source document as Russian KCTG missile activity.
2. U.S. P-8A patrols in the Eastern Mediterranean during this period were specifically tasked to monitor Russian naval and air activity.
3. The geographic pattern (observations clustered along Russian naval transit routes and near Russian-aligned Syrian airfields) supports the linkage.
