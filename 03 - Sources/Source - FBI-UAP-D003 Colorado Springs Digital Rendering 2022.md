## Summary

A single-page digital rendering reconstructing the February 2022 Cheyenne Mountain object from witness testimony and the forensic sketch.

## Visual description (viewed)

The image shows a clear daytime blue sky over the forested Cheyenne Mountain massif, with the Fort Carson / Colorado Springs suburbs and a highway guardrail in the foreground. Hovering above the ridgeline is a horizontal, lozenge/bean-shaped object with a milky, semi-translucent white surface textured in fine intersecting scale-like panels. It has soft defined edges and a faint shimmer, consistent with the witnesses' "off-white, opalescent, fish-scale panel" descriptions. The object is the only anomalous element; lighting and shadow on it match the daytime scene.
