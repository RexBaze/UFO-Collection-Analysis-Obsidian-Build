## Assessment

==Unresolved, analytically notable.== The timing is the striking element: an object transited the targeting sensor in the seconds between weapon release and impact, captured on two independent MX-series sensors, with no other known aircraft in the airspace. That rules out a simple display artifact and a known cooperative aircraft, while leaving small-UAS or debris as conventional candidates. The dual-sensor IR detection gives this more evidentiary weight than the typical single-feed call.
