## Summary

FBI FD-302 witness interview with a second member of the LiDAR test convoy. Witness was driving the lead vehicle (an F-150) and saw a bright white light over the horizon at approximately 9:00 AM. Light was stationary, then moved right and disappeared after about ten seconds. Witness self-attributed the observation as a probable meteor.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "The vehicles drove through a couple of gates and [witness] saw a bright light over the horizon. The light was stationary in the air, then started moving to the right and then disappeared. [Witness] could see the light through the top right of the vehicle's windshield. The light was bright white and was visible for ten seconds before it disappeared. The light stayed the same size throughout the incident. [Witness] thought the light was ten to twenty miles away. [Witness] did not notice any interference with his vehicle."

> [!QUOTE]
> "[Witness] thought the light might have been a meteor coming straight toward them and burning up in the atmosphere."
