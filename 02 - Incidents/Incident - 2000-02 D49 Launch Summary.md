## Narrative

This is a historical launch catalog, not a UAP observation:

> [!QUOTE]
> VANDENBERG AFB LAUNCH SUMMARY 1958 - 2000. 30th SPACE WING, Office of History.

Its value is as a cross-reference: many nocturnal 'UFO' reports over the western United States coincide with rocket launches, reentries, and fuel dumps. A complete launch list supports conventional attribution of date-matched sightings.
