## Analytical note

Two threads run through the series: (1) the Cold War instrumentalization of UFO data as Soviet/Chinese aerospace intelligence (006, 008, 011, 012, 016), and (2) the official US/allied investigative apparatus and its debunking turn (002, 007, 014, 015, 019). The U-2/OXCART monograph (003) is the explicit institutional admission that classified reconnaissance generated many "UFO" reports.
