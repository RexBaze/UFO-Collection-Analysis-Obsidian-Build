## Assessment

==Unresolved.== An 8-minute observation with multiple abrupt directional changes is unusually long and unusually maneuver-rich for the collection. Combined with the matching D44 report from the same squadron one month later, this suggests a ==recurring class of objects operating in the Gulf of Aden== in late 2020.

Candidate explanations:

1. ==Houthi UAS== (Yemen-launched, possibly Iran-supplied; abrupt direction changes consistent with active drone navigation)
2. Small unmanned aircraft of unspecified origin
3. Persistent natural phenomenon limited to certain weather conditions in that geography
