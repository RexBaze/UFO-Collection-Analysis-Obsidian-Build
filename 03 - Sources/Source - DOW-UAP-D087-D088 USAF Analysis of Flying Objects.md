## Summary

A two-volume USAF compilation, "Analysis of Flying Objects in the US" - standardized per-incident worksheets (location, observer, altitude, speed, color, maneuver, weather) for numbered cases 1-100 (D087) and 101-172 (D088). These are the primary case files feeding the Project Sign/Grudge statistical effort that culminated in Blue Book Special Report No. 14 ([[Source - CIA-UAP Series Index|CIA-UAP-015]]). They correspond to the "Incident Summaries" series already catalogued in [[Historical Archive Index]].

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> (D087, early Muroc AAF case) "the objects were not weather balloons since they traveled against the wind."

> [!QUOTE]
> (D088, Case 101, 18 Feb 1948 Decatur KS) a citizen speculated "the moon is either inhabited or in use by other than human beings."

Range spans confirmed meteors/meteorites through credible spherical/disc reports judged not balloons, birds, or optical illusions.
