## Significance

Royal Jordanian Air Force base hosting U.S. and coalition fighter and ISR aircraft for [[Operation Inherent Resolve]]. Home station during 332 AEW deployments. Launching airfield for [[Incident - 2023-02-21 Shaddadi F-15E]] and similar Syria DCA sorties.
