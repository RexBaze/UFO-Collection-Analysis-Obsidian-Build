## Assessment

==Unresolved, analytically significant.== Two features lift this above the routine ISR calls: a very high estimated altitude (FL600+) and an explicit analyst control test, the crew compared the targeting-pod signature of a candidate object against a known star and found them different, which argues against a pure optical/atmospheric artifact. The large count and fast motion remain unexplained from this document alone.
