## Numbering observation

==All 28 IDs fall in the narrow range 111688723 to 111689759== (a span of 1036). This is consistent with a single batch upload to the DOD video catalog system, not 28 unrelated releases. The IDs are sequential within sub-clusters (e.g., 111688723/762/775/809/816/825 are close; 111689005/011/022/030/044/051/057 are close; 111689082/083 are adjacent suggesting cuts from the same source clip).

The natural hypothesis is that this set is the bulk video accompaniment to the DOW MISREP and Range Fouler releases, with each video being the sensor recording referenced by `Video footage can be found at this link` in the corresponding MISREP. The redacted links in the MISREPs almost certainly point to specific catalog IDs in this set.
