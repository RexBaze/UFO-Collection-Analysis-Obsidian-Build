## Notes

==This is one of the most credible single-witness sets in the entire collection.== Captain Rhodes is named in the State Department cable (a 1994-era diplomatic record is unlikely to attribute statements falsely to a named individual). He took photographs with a personal Olympus camera and stated his opinion that the object was extraterrestrial and under intelligent control, with apparent crew concurrence. The cable's drafting officer (Escudero, Embassy Dushanbe) carefully reported the observation while declining to endorse the interpretation.

The combination of (a) named, identifiable witness, (b) multiple corroborating observers, (c) photographic record, (d) physical residue (contrails), and (e) the witness's own application and rejection of conventional explanations places this incident at a higher evidentiary tier than most observations in the collection.
