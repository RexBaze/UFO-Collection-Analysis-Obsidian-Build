## Summary

A CIA Information Report tabulating sightings of bright, fast-moving objects over South Ladakh, NE Nepal, North Sikkim, and Western Bhutan between 19 February and 25 March 1968. Witnesses described rocket-like objects trailing white-yellow smoke at 20,000-25,000 ft, bluish high-speed objects bright enough to illuminate the ground, and thunder/blast sounds, with flight directions generally trending from the direction of Tibet (NE to SW). The report relays an alleged recovery of a "huge metallic disc-shaped object" fragment in a crater near Pokhara, Nepal. Filed through the lens of Cold War interest in Chinese missile/nuclear activity.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
