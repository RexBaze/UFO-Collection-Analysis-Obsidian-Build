## Assessment

==Probable conventional (balloon).== Critical attributes:

- Drifting with the winds (not powered)
- 31,000 ft is within standard weather balloon and surveillance balloon operating altitudes
- Weapons quality 1 radar track (would not be typical of low-RCS exotic platform)
- Crew themselves identified it as balloon-like
- Cross-referenced with prior 48FW report (RAF Lakenheath F-15E unit, UK)

The 48FW reference is interesting: it suggests an established pattern of balloon-like UAP reports across multiple wings, which the crews are now recognizing as a recurring (likely conventional) phenomenon.
