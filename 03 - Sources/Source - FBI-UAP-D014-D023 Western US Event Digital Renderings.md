## Summary

Ten AI-generated digital renderings reconstructing the Western U.S. Event from agent testimony, grouped as Incident 1 (D014, D015, D021) and Incident 2 (D016-D020, D022, D023). Like the notional map, these are illustrative reconstructions, not photographs.

## Visual description (viewed)

Incident 1 renderings (D014, D015, D021): a desert road at deep dusk leading toward low hills. A bright orange "mother orb" hovers near a ridge on the right, with a short horizontal line of small red orbs beside it (the "launching" sequence). D021 (1-3) shows the large orange orb alone, glowing over a layered sandstone bluff beside a curving road.

Incident 2 renderings (D016-D020, D022, D023): two agent silhouettes (outlined with a faint glow) stand on a rise watching a starry desert sky. A light on the right horizon brightens across the sequence - a pale yellow dot, then a yellow dot with a red center, then a yellow orb trailing a horizontal row of three, then four, red dots. D022 shows the in-vehicle view: two occupants and a pale glowing orb hovering over the road ahead through the windshield. D023 is the most dramatic: two agents beside a vehicle (taillight visible) watch an arc of ~6 red orbs above a mountain ridge while two pairs of white vehicle-like lights sit on the distant desert floor.
