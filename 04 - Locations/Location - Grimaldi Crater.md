coordinates:: ~5.5 S, 68.3 W (lunar selenographic)

## Significance

Large flooded impact basin on the lunar near side. Location of Apollo 17 transient flash observation reported by LMP Jack Schmitt during translunar coast. Site of multiple historical Transient Lunar Phenomena reports in the broader astronomical literature.
