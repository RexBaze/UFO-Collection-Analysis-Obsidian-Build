## Geography

country:: International waters between Yemen and Somalia
region:: Horn of Africa
aor:: centcom

## Significance

Critical maritime chokepoint at the entrance to the Red Sea (Bab al-Mandeb). Routine U.S. and coalition naval patrols (CTF-150, CTF-151). Active threat environment due to Houthi anti-shipping operations from Yemen.
