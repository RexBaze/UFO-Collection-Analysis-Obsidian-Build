## What it is

Operation Inherent Resolve (OIR) is the U.S. and coalition military operation against the Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS), formally named in October 2014. It operates across the USCENTCOM area of responsibility, primarily Iraq and Syria, under Combined Joint Task Force - Operation Inherent Resolve (CJTF-OIR).

## Relevance to this collection

OIR is the operational backdrop for several CENTCOM UAP observations in the vault. Aircraft and units tasked to the counter-ISIS mission were the observers in a number of incidents, and the dense, sensor-rich coalition air picture over Iraq and Syria is part of why these events were captured and reported at all.
