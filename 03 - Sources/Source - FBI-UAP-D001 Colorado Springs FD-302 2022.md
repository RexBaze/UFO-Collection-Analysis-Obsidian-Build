## Summary

FBI FD-302 record of a March 2025 Microsoft Teams interview with one of the five Fort Carson witnesses to the February 2022 Cheyenne Mountain sighting. The witness describes a matte/off-white, non-metallic, oval "bean-shaped" object with a curved indentation, completely motionless in a saddle of the mountain, silent, observed for ~3-5 minutes by all five soldiers. The object was covered in intersecting lines forming an abstract polygon pattern and was about the size of a pool cue held at arm's length. None had a phone; when they looked away to fetch one, the object had vanished.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "matte white, or off-white in color and appearing non-metallic... oval shaped and in a horizontal position, with a curved indentation on the bottom."

> [!QUOTE]
> "Each of them independently drew what they remembered the object to look like... All of the drawings were consistent."

The witness stated he was contacted by AARO in early 2024 but "was never interviewed by AARO or anyone else" before this FBI engagement.
