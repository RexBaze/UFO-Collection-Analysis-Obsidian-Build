## Notes

The primary witness reports recurring activity at a Northeastern US residence from November 2021 onward, captured on video in October 2024 and described again in February 2026 FD-302 interviews: a ~1-meter red sphere with a basketball-sized "white plasma sun" core, joined by a second identical orb, the two moving west in tandem before merging into one. A second witness (FD-302-71) described yellow basketball-to-beach-ball-sized balls of light ~30 yards out at 20-30 ft altitude, with one orb "going into the other orb before disappearing," and recounted an earlier 1987 power-line sighting. The witnesses used ADS-B data themselves to rule out conventional aircraft and offered to host FBI monitoring equipment.
