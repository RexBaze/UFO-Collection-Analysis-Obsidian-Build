## Narrative

A 2-ship of [[Platform - F-15E Strike Eagle|F-15E]] from [[Unit - 389 EFS|389 EFS]], [[Unit - 332 AEW|332 AEW]] launched from [[Location - Muwaffaq Salti Air Base|Muwaffaq Salti Air Base (OJMS)]] in support of [[Operation Inherent Resolve]] to conduct Defensive Counter-Air in the ESSA killbox over eastern [[Location - Shaddadi|Shaddadi, Syria]]. While cruising in CAP at FL270, the flight received three minutes of MFT radar jamming on the [[Sensor - APG-82 Radar|APG-82]] (frequencies 8.8 to 9.9 GHz, NTFR). The working theory in the report assesses the jamming as a Turkish X-band ground emitter near the Syria/Turkey border, ==not UAP-caused==.

One minute after the jamming ended, the crew observed 3 possible UAPs at FL240. The Weather Sensor Video (WSV) was produced. No radar returns were received from the objects. No health effects were experienced by aircrew.

> [!QUOTE]
> "0025Z- OBS 3X POSS UAP IVO SHADDADI AT FL240. WSV PRODUCED. NFTR. WAS CRUSING IN CAP AT FL270. NO RADAR RETURNS RECEIVED FROM UAP. NO HEALTH EFFECTS EXPERIENCED BY AIRCREW. 2 WHITE OBJECTS IR SIGNIFICANT. NFTR."

A second AIRSIGHT event followed at 0135Z: 1x possible balloon at FL210, IVO Shaddadi. This event is identified as a balloon in the report.

## Assessment

The 3 objects observed at 0025Z remain ==unresolved==. The proximate radar jamming event has a non-UAP attribution in the same report. The objects had no radar return, no IFF, and the visual observation was brief in cloudy weather. The 0135Z balloon sighting in the same area within the same sortie is a useful baseline showing that conventional balloons are also present in this corridor, which complicates clean attribution.

The Syria/Iraq border corridor is heavily trafficked by ISR balloons, surveillance aerostats, Turkish drones, and Russian aircraft, and remains an area of active hostilities.
