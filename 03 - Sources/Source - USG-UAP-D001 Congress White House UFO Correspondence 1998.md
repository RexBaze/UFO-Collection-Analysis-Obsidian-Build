## Summary

An 86-page file of 1998 NASA Headquarters correspondence answering constituent UFO letters routed via Sen. Olympia Snowe, Sen. Charles Grassley, Rep. Bart Gordon, and a White House referral (concerning possible UFO hearings and a CSETI member). NASA's stock reply (signed Edward Heffernan, Legislative Affairs) enclosed a fact sheet and James Oberg's debunking article. Notably, this is the one tranche 3 NASA-related file where the canonical astronaut "UFO" sightings actually appear - quoted and then explained as boosters, debris, or photo artifacts.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "NASA has no program for investigating UFOs and has not withheld information on sightings."

The file reproduces the verbatim Gemini 7 "got a bogey at 10 o'clock high" exchange, the McDivitt Gemini-4 "unidentified spacecraft," and Cooper's green-object claim - then debunks each. This is why those legends are absent from the primary debrief transcripts ([[Source - NASA Gemini Debriefings Index]]) but famous in the public record.
