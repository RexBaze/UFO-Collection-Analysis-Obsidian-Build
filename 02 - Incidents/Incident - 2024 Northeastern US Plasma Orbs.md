## Narrative

Following an August 2024 referral, the FBI investigated recurring "plasma orb" activity at a private Northeastern U.S. residence (sightings since November 2021). During a November 2024 site visit, FBI special agents personally observed pulsating lights: a white pulsation moving horizontally and erratically below the treeline, treeline white/blue pulsing lights, and - at 5:30pm - "a bright white light above a bright red light" hovering at treetop height and darting right-to-left twice. Five photos were taken, mostly blurry long exposures. A December 2024 daylight perimeter survey found nothing unusual at ground level, and agents judged drone flight through the dense treeline implausible.

## Assessment

==Unresolved==. The strongest element is direct FBI agent observation of pulsating white/red lights on site, plus the agents' assessment that drone flight through the wooded area was implausible. Counterweights: photography was poor (long-exposure blur can manufacture "orbs"), and the witness's extraordinary claims (gamma-radiation spikes, GPS/electronics anomalies, "people have been hurt") are unverified and rest on self-report. Conventional candidates include drones with illuminated payloads, pyrotechnics/lanterns, and camera artifacts, none cleanly fitting the agents' direct observation.
