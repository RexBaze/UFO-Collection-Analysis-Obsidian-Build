## Significance

On the afternoon of 2 July 2008, an unidentified object was reported hovering at high altitude directly over Harare International Airport, possibly detected by both radar and optical means. The object was described as disc-like with a hollow center and rotating underside lights that shifted colors before the object ascended rapidly out of visual range. The incident was reported in a SECRET//NOFORN CIA cable distributed to a wide U.S. military and intelligence audience (including the White House Situation Room) and contributed to a decision to place Zimbabwe on high alert.

This is the only Zimbabwe entry in the collection and one of the few hard "disc with structure" sightings with claimed dual-phenomenology (radar plus optical) corroboration.
