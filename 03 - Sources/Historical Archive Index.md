## Notes on archival reference number patterns

- `18_` prefix: Army records (NARA RG 18, U.S. Air Force)
- `38_` prefix: Records of the Department of the Air Force (NARA RG 341 likely)
- `59_` prefix: ==State Department records== (NARA RG 59)
- `65_` prefix: ==FBI records== (NARA RG 65)
- `255_` prefix: NASA records (NARA RG 255)
- `331_` prefix: Records of Allied Operational and Occupation Headquarters, WWII (NARA RG 331)
- `341_` prefix: Records of the Department of the Air Force (NARA RG 341)
- `342_` prefix: Records of the United States Air Force Commands, Activities, and Organizations (NARA RG 342)
- `HS1` infix: Holdings Series 1 (NARA finding aid convention)

These NARA Record Group numbers tell you the provenance and finding-aid context. Cross-referencing against the [National Archives Catalog](https://catalog.archives.gov/) for the same identifiers should reveal the full archival metadata.
