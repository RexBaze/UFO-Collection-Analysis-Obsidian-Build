## Narrative

> [!QUOTE]
> Reported Appearance: An individual reported observing a large blue featureless triangular object with a solid, unwavering silhouette emitting powerful "whitish blue" light from multiple points along its perimeter.

> [!QUOTE]
> Reported Behavior: The reporter described the object as "hovering" stationary above or near a national security facility for approximately three minutes. They then reported observing it move to a position higher in their field of view, characterizing the motion as "backing up" in a "jerking" or "jumping" manner inconsistent with "smooth" jet propulsion. The individual reported observing the object for approximately eight minutes.

## Assessment

==Unresolved.== A classic triangular-craft report, notable for the law-enforcement/counterintelligence reporting channel (AFOSI) and the proximity to a national security facility rather than a battlefield. The hovering followed by jerking vertical movement is the reported anomaly. As a single-witness visual-only account it carries the usual eyewitness limits, and conventional candidates (drones, aircraft lighting) cannot be excluded. Geographically this belongs to the domestic CONUS reporting trend, not INDOPACOM; the location has been corrected accordingly with the precise site withheld.
