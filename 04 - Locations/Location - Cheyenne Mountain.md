## Significance

One of the densest military and space-domain sites in the continental United States. Cheyenne Mountain hosts the hardened Cheyenne Mountain Complex (the NORAD/USNORTHCOM alternate command center). The adjacent [[Location - Western United States|Front Range corridor]] includes Fort Carson (4th Infantry Division), Peterson and Schriever Space Force Bases, the U.S. Air Force Academy, and the headquarters of U.S. Space Command. The airspace is busy, heavily instrumented, and routinely monitored.

The 15 February 2022 sighting was reported by five U.S. Army service members at Fort Carson who observed a stationary, shape-shifting white object over the mountain silhouette. It is the first domestic (NORTHCOM-AOR) incident entered into this collection and the first to originate as an FBI rather than a military mission report.
