## Summary

A 1949 Department of the Army General Staff study (Plans & Operations Division, decimal file P&O 350.05), "Evaluation Study of Phenomenon (Flying Saucers)." P&O requested an evaluation, routed through the Intelligence Division, to determine whether saucer reports stemmed from natural phenomena or from a foreign power, referencing radios from Kirtland AFB and Wright-Patterson AFB (AMC). Signed by Col. John H. Guthrie, Executive, P&O. Contemporaneous with USAF Project Grudge; documents inter-service handling of the saucer question.
