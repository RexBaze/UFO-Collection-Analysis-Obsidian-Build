> [!NOTE]
> The 32 "FBI Photo" files (A1-A8 PNG, B1-B24 PDF) are sensor frames, not stand-alone investigative photographs. Each frame shows a centered crosshair reticle, hash-mark scale, redaction bars over sensor metadata, and a single dark or bright object near center. The visual style is consistent with U.S. military targeting pod imagery (ATFLIR, Sniper-SE), not FBI investigative photography. The "FBI" label is likely a filing or release-channel convention.
