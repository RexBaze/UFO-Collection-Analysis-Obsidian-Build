## Summary

Mission report covering a 2-ship [[Platform - F-15E Strike Eagle]] DCA sortie from [[Location - Muwaffaq Salti Air Base]] (OJMS) over the ESSA killbox in eastern [[Location - Shaddadi|Shaddadi, Syria]] on the night of 20-21 February 2023, in support of [[Operation Inherent Resolve]]. The report documents an EMI event (3 minutes of MFT radar jamming on the APG-82) and two AIRSIGHT events: 3 possible UAPs at FL240 (1 minute after jamming ended) and 1 possible balloon at FL210 approximately one hour later.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> "0021Z-0024Z RECEIVED MFT RADAR JAMMING IVO SHADDADI AT FL270. NTFR. THIS EVENT IS STANDARD ON AZIMUTH, MFT RADAR JAMMING EXPERIENCED BY AIRCREW NORTH OF SHADDADI. OUR WORKING THEORY IS THAT IT IS AN AERA OF EFFECT TURKISH X-BAND JAMMER ON OR ACROSS THE SYR/TUR BORDER INTO TURKEY."

> [!QUOTE]
> "0025Z OBS 3X POSS UAP IVO SHADDADI AT FL240. WSV PRODUCED. NFTR. WAS CRUSING IN CAP AT FL270. NO RADAR RETURNS RECEIVED FROM UAP. NO HEALTH EFFECTS EXPERIENCED BY AIRCREW. 2 WHITE OBJECTS IR SIGNIFICANT. NFTR."
