## Summary

The 13 March 1953 transmittal record of the CIA Scientific Advisory Panel (Robertson Panel) report to the Secretary of Defense, via a SecDef routing slip. A cover letter from Richard D. Drain (Secretary, Intelligence Advisory Committee) furnishes the report, convened at the direction of DCI Walter Bedell Smith. The panel (chaired by H.P. Robertson; Lloyd Berkner referenced) found UFOs posed no direct threat but warned of indirect "potential dangers to national security," and recommended public de-emphasis - shaping Blue Book policy for a decade. This is the DOW-held transmittal copy; the CIA-held report itself is [[Source - CIA-UAP-002 Robertson Panel Report]].

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> the panel pointed out "certain potential dangers to national security."

> [!QUOTE]
> "this Agency does not consider problems arising from sightings of 'flying saucers'... primarily its concern."
