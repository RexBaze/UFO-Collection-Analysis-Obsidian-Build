## Summary

Five FBI FD-1057 electronic communications documenting the Bureau's 2024 investigation into recurring "plasma orb" sightings at a private Northeastern U.S. residence (occupied since 2006; first sighting Nov 2021). Same case ID and witness recur throughout. D004 records initial contact (witness referred by an FBI liaison in Aug 2024; orbs reported as close as 200 yards, with claimed gamma-radiation spikes and GPS/electronics anomalies). D005 is the witness interview (a 2021 light split into 2-3 forming a triangular pattern; a recounted 1996 New Mexico vehicle-shutdown event; ADS-B used to rule out aircraft). D006 is a site survey (trail camera placed ~200 yards into a restricted wooded tract). D007 is the key document: FBI agents personally observed pulsating lights. D008 is a daylight perimeter survey finding nothing unusual at ground level.

## Key extractions

> [!QUOTE]
> (D007) two agents saw "a bright white light above a bright red light" hovering at treetop height, darting right-to-left twice.

> [!QUOTE]
> (D005) the witness asserted "people have been hurt by these things."

> [!QUOTE]
> (D008) agents assessed it "unrealistic for someone to fly a drone... without... crashing into tree limbs."
