## What it is

SPEAR is the U.S. Navy analysis cell within the Office of Naval Intelligence (ONI) to which Navy "Range Fouler" UAP debrief forms are routed for analysis. The acronym is commonly reported as Strike Projection Evaluation and Anti-surface warfare Research; treat the expansion as reported rather than confirmed from the released documents.

## Relevance to this collection

In the D56 Range Fouler debrief (an MH-60R pilot's report of three unidentified small air contacts in the North Arabian Sea, August 2020), the standard Navy debrief form is described as routed to SPEAR for analysis. SPEAR is therefore the Navy-side analytic destination for structured range-fouler reporting, analogous in function to how CENTCOM MISREP and Range Fouler reporting feeds into the broader UAP analysis chain.
